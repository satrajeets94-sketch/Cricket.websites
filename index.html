My Hobby – Cricket

1. What is Cricket?

Cricket is a popular sport played between two teams using a bat and ball. It is known for teamwork, strategy, and excitement, making it one of the most loved games in the world.

2. Why Cricket is My Favourite Hobby

Cricket keeps me active and disciplined. It helps me stay fit, improves my focus, and gives me confidence. Playing or watching cricket always makes me happy.

3. My Favourite Players

My favourite players are Virat Kohli, MS Dhoni, and Rohit Sharma. Their dedication, sportsmanship, and passion inspire young players like me.

4. Types of Cricket Matches

Cricket is played in different formats such as Test Matches, One Day Internationals, and T20 Matches. Each format is exciting in its own way.

5. What Cricket Teaches Me

Cricket teaches discipline, patience, teamwork, leadership, and how to handle pressure. It helps me stay positive and motivates me to work harder every day.

6. My Experience Playing Cricket

I enjoy batting, bowling, and fielding with my friends. Every match teaches me something new. Winning increases confidence and losing teaches important life lessons.

7. Why Everyone Should Try Cricket

Cricket keeps the mind sharp and the body active. It builds strength, improves coordination, and teaches how to work as a team.
