My Hobby – Cricket

1. What is Cricket?

Cricket is a widely popular sport played between two teams with bat and ball. It involves strategy, teamwork, fitness, and skill development.

2. Why I Love Cricket

I love cricket because it keeps me active, makes me disciplined, and improves my focus. Playing matches with friends gives me joy and confidence.

3. My Favourite Player

My favourite cricketer is Virat Kohli. His dedication, fitness, and aggression inspire me to work hard and perform better every day.

4. Cricket Equipments

Cricket needs basic equipment like bat, ball, gloves, pads, stumps, and helmet. These protect the player and help improve performance.

5. What I Learn from Cricket

Cricket teaches discipline, patience, team coordination, and quick decision making. It also teaches how to handle pressure and stay calm in tough situations.
