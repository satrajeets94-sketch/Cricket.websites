My Hobby – Cricket

About Cricket

Cricket is my favourite sport. I enjoy both batting and bowling. It teaches discipline, teamwork, and focus. Watching cricket matches also helps me learn new techniques, strategies, and skills.

Why I Love Cricket

Cricket keeps me active, improves fitness, and builds confidence. Playing with friends makes me happy and reduces stress. I follow international matches and support my favourite players.
